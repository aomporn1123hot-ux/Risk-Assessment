Instructions

This program is used for self-assessment of work posture risk according to FERA guidelines.

How to use

  1. Read each question and select the answer that best matches your posture
  2. Illustrations are provided to help you decide
  3. After completing all questions, the system will show your score and risk level

The results are preliminary assessment only to help improve your work posture.
This is not a medical diagnosis.
